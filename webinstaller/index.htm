ESPGeiger Gadget web installer

Flash version {version} straight from your browser.

  1. Plug the Cheap Yellow Display into a USB port.
  2. Choose your panel below. Not sure? Count the USB ports: two ports (one is USB-C) is ST7789, a single micro-USB is ILI9341.
  3. Hit "Install" and pick the serial port. No device found?
  4. Wait for the flash to finish (faster if you stay on this tab), then follow the on-screen WiFi setup.

This erases any existing firmware and settings on the board.
