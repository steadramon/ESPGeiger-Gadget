ESPGeiger Gadget {version}
  1. Plug the Cheap Yellow Display into a USB port.
  2. Choose your panel below. Not sure? Count the USB ports: two ports (one is USB-C) is ST7789, a single micro-USB is ILI9341.
  3. Click Install and pick the serial port. No device found?
  4. Wait while ESPGeiger Gadget {version} flashes (faster if you stay on this tab).
  5. When the gadget reboots, follow the on-screen WiFi setup.

Note: This erases any existing firmware and settings on the board.
